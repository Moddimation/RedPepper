    An in-progress decompilation of Super Mario 3D Land for the Nintendo 3DS.
    The focus is currently laid on the European release, but multiversion groundwork was made for all available versions.

    There are no known symbol maps for this game, however, you can use affiliated games, such as: - Super Mario Galaxy: The game was based on SMG2, and while we dont have its symbol map, we have that of SMG1. It was put into al:: - Captain Toad Treasure Tracker (SW): Switch version provides symbols, while the direct 3DS version does not. - Super Mario 3D World (SW): It is a mix of Super Mario Odyssey and Super Mario 3D World, which came after 3DL. - Super Mario Odyssey: Has a full function map, including private functions. For SEAD you can explore debugging.games for 3DS games with sead or just use pead from libpia_pead.a
